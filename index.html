Hacker

A hacker is a person who uses computers to gain unauthorized access to YOUR data. They use various techniques and ways to trick you into allowing them to access your computer information which allows them to view a lot of your personal data/information

The best way to avoid these hackers are to avoid fishy emails, QR codes, links or anything you may encounter over the internet that seems like it could be a virus. It will be easy to tell what websites are legitimate and which are not. When in doubt click out.
